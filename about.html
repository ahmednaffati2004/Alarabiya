Who We Are?

Alarabiya is a Libyan construction company governed with highly qualified & expert Libyan staff, located in Tripoli. We have been providing integrated technical and practical expertise in Project Management, Construction, Consultancy, Engineering, Facility Management & Procurement Services since 2001. We have extensive experience with engineering and construction works and related technical consultancy from small to large scale tum key & EPC projects. In case of strategic projects, we work with our partners specialized in that field.

Our Vision

Our main goal is to provide our client with the best solution and professional experience that will meet their needs and satisfaction We offer a comprehensive range of services throughout project life cycle:

  • Feasibility Studies
  • Conceptual Designs
  • Project Management
  • Engineering (Front end & detailed)
  • Procurement
  • Construction Supervision and Commissioning
  • Quality Control
  • Quality Assurance
